Frequently Asked Questions

What is SEO and why does it matter? +
SEO helps your website show up on search engines like Google when people search for your services. More visibility leads to more traffic and potential customers.
How long does SEO take to show results? +
SEO is a long-term game. You can see improvements in weeks, but strong results usually build over 2–3 months and keep growing consistently.
Is SEO better than paid ads? +
Ads give quick results, while SEO builds long-term, consistent traffic without paying for every single click. Both can work together.
