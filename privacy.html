Privacy Policy

At Mark Next Media, your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

A. Personal Information

When you:

  • Fill out a contact form

  • Book a consultation

  • Subscribe to newsletters

  • Request a proposal

  • Work with us as a client

We may collect:

  • Full Name

  • Email Address

  • Phone Number

  • Company Name

  • Billing Details

  • Project Information

B. Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP Address

  • Browser Type

  • Device Information

  • Pages Visited

  • Time Spent on Website

  • Referral Source

This helps us improve performance and user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide support

  • Deliver our digital marketing, web development, and Web 3 services

  • Improve website functionality and user experience

  • Run marketing campaigns.

  • We do NOT sell data to third parties.

  • Send updates, newsletters, or promotional material (only if you opt-in).

  • Analyze performance and optimize strategies.

  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Improve browsing experience

  • Analyze website traffic

  • Track campaign performance

  • Personalize content

  • You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not function properly.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party tools such as:

  • Analytics tools

  • Payment gateways

  • CRM systems

  • Advertising platforms

  • Email marketing platforms

These third parties may collect information as per their own privacy policies. We ensure we work only with reputable and secure platforms.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from:

  • Unauthorized access

  • Data misuse

  • Alteration

  • Disclosure

While we strive to protect your data, no online system is 100% secure.

6. Client Data & Confidentiality

For clients working with us:

  • All project-related data is kept confidential.

  • We do not share proprietary information, login credentials, or campaign data without written consent.

  • Access is limited to authorized team members only.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill service obligations

  • Comply with legal requirements

  • Resolve disputes

  • You may request deletion of your data at any time (subject to legal obligations).

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data

  • Correct inaccurate information

  • Request deletion of your data

  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications

  • Request data portability

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with the updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
